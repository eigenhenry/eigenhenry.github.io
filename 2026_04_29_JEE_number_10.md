This study examines the strategic actions of engi-neering instructional faculty to support their students' experiences at Hispanic-serving institutions (HSIs). Particularly, it seeks to identify the factorsinfluencing faculty actions using the Contextualized Theory of ProfessionalAgency for Impact in Engineering Education.
